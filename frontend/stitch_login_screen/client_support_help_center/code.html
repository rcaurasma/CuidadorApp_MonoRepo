receipt_long Billing & Insurance

How do I generate a monthly invoice for a patient? expand_more

Navigate to the 'Patients' tab, select the specific patient profile, and click on 'Financial History'. From there, use the 'Generate Invoice' button on the top right corner. You can select the date range and include specific services.

Can I update insurance details in bulk? expand_more

Currently, bulk updates for sensitive insurance information are disabled for security compliance. Please update each patient's insurance details individually through their profile settings page.

What to do if a claim is rejected? expand_more

Check the rejection code in the 'Claims' dashboard. Common errors include incorrect policy numbers or mismatched service dates. Correct the data and use the 'Resubmit' feature. If the issue persists, contact the provider directly.

medication Care Services & Scheduling

How to assign multiple caregivers to a single shift? expand_more

In the Schedule view, click on the shift slot. In the 'Assign Staff' modal, you can select multiple caregivers by holding 'Ctrl' (or 'Cmd' on Mac) while clicking names. Ensure all selected staff have availability.

Where can I find the patient medication logs? expand_more

Medication logs are located within the Patient Profile under the 'Health Records' tab. Logs are updated in real-time by caregivers via the mobile app.
